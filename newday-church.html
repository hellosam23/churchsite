Good to know

Common questions

I'm not religious. Is that okay?

Completely. Plenty of people here are exploring, questioning, or just curious. You're welcome exactly as you are — no pressure, no expectations.

Will I be singled out or asked to give?

No. We won't put you on the spot or ask first-time guests to give. Just relax and take it in.

What about my kids?

Check-in opens 20 minutes before each service. Our team is background-checked and trained, and every child is signed in and out securely.
